Doordarshan Special Bulletin

DD NATIONAL • RETRO SCAN ● LIVE INTERVIEW

विशेष बुलेटिन: जाँच से पुष्टि हुई है कि आपका दिल पुराने कैसेट टेप, किशोर कुमार के गानों और यश चोपड़ा की बरसाती फ़िल्मों के बीच धड़कता है।

कुछ ख़ास बातें जो मैंने देखीं:
  • • तुम्हें पुराने गानों के एक-एक शब्द ज़ुबानी याद हैं।
  • • तुम किसी भी थीम के लिए बेहद सलीके और पूरी शिद्दत से तैयार होती हो।
  • • जब भी तुम पुरानी फ़िल्मों की बात करती हो, तुम्हारी आँखें चमक उठती हैं।
  • • तुम्हारा आज भी मानना है कि हाथ से लिखी पुरानी चीज़ों में एक जादुई एहसास होता है।
  • • जिन चीज़ों को लोग अक्सर अनदेखा कर गुज़र जाते हैं, तुम उनमें भी कहानियाँ और ख़ूबसूरती ढूँढ लेती हो।
05 JUL 2026 AIR MAIL

Dearest Snehal,

People often say that everyone belongs to the time they are born in. But honestly, I've always felt that some people are exceptions. And I think you're one of them.

In a world where everything changes so quickly, you've somehow managed to hold on to things that never really go out of style — old songs, meaningful stories, beautiful lyrics, and the kind of emotions that stay with us long after the movie ends or the music stops.

I've always found it fascinating how much you genuinely love old Bollywood. You don't just listen to those songs because they're popular classics, and you don't watch those movies just for entertainment. You enjoy the feelings, the stories, the poetry, and the little details that make that era so special.

Maybe that's why I've always felt that you carry a little bit of that golden era within you. The warmth, the simplicity, and the timeless charm that people still miss today.

So on your birthday, I just want to wish that you never lose this part of yourself. Keep loving old songs, keep getting excited about classic movies, and keep being exactly the person you are.

Wishing you a birthday filled with happiness, good memories, beautiful music, and everything that makes your heart feel at home.

Happy Birthday. And honestly, if there really was an alternate universe somewhere in the 70s or 80s, I'm pretty sure you would've been one of its brightest stars. ✨

From someone who believes you were born in the right world, but perhaps the wrong decade.
